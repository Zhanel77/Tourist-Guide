Question

1. How can I book a hotel and tour date?

You can book a hotel and tour through travel agencies. - Travel agencies: many offer package tours where you can immediately choose a hotel and tour with included flights and excursions. On our website you will find the "Hotel" and "Transport" tabs where you can also book a hotel and date - Hotel websites: you can also choose dates and book a room directly on the hotel website.

2. Where is the best place to go on holiday in winter?

- Both warm and snowy destinations are popular for winter holidays: - Warm countries: - The Maldives, Thailand, and the UAE are ideal places for a beach holiday in winter with warm seas and wonderful weather. - Ski resorts: - Switzerland, Austria, and France (the Alps) are great places for winter sports and mountain recreation.

3. Where can I go with my family?

- The following destinations are perfect for a family vacation: - Spain (Costa Brava, Canary Islands) — warm sea, entertainment for children and adults. - France (Disneyland in Paris) — an ideal place for children. - Turkey (Antalya, Kemer) — many family hotels with animation and water parks.

4. What are the best destinations for active tourism?

Nepal (Himalayas, trekking), New Zealand (hiking, water sports), Norway (trekking in the fjords), Peru (trekking to Machu Picchu), Canada (mountaineering, hiking in national parks) and Iceland (glacial caves, geysers) Popular among active holiday lovers.

5. Which countries are best for solo travel and which for family travel?

Japan, New Zealand, Canada, Portugal and Iceland are considered safe and convenient for solo travel. With family travel, it is better to travel to countries with developed infrastructure and entertainment, such as Spain, Italy, France or Australia.

6. Which countries are the safest for tourists in 2024?

The safest countries in 2024 include Iceland, New Zealand, Japan, Portugal, Switzerland, Austria, and Denmark. These countries have low crime rates and stable political environments.
